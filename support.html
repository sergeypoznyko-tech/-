FAQ & Troubleshooting

Does the app collect any data? No

We do not collect, store, or share personal data. See the Privacy Policy page for details.

The app behaves unexpectedly. What should I do?

Try these steps:
1) Close the app and reopen
2) Restart the device
3) Make sure iOS is up to date
4) Reinstall the app (if needed)

I found a bug. How can I report it?

Email support with:
• iPhone/iPad model
• iOS version
• Steps to reproduce
• Screenshots (optional)

Feature request / feedback
